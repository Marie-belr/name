L'humanité

This video was made for a challenge at ISCOM. It is the 24h créa challenge of 2020. The 24h créa are made once a year and gather the students of first, second and third year. The goal of this challenge is to make a short film in 24 hours. The challenge starts at 10 am and the rendering is done at 10 am the next day. We work on it all day and all night. The subject which was proposed to us this year was: "make a HYMN TO HUMANITY". In this challenge, I was appointed project manager. I wrote the script with my team. I was then director and cameraman and assisted for the light and the sound by my team. I then realized all the video editing.

Les dangers de l'alcool

This video was made for the 24h crea challenge of 2019 at ISCOM. The principle of this challenge is described under the video « l'humanité". For this challenge, our advertiser was Santé Publique France and we had to make a preventive advert to prevent from the dangers of alcohol among young people. For this video, I participated in the writing of the script and I was then co-actor of the film. Then I realized all the editing of the video. Thanks to this short film, we were elected favorite of the jury.
